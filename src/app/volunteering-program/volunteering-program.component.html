1. Media and communication

We need volunteers who can help our NGO with all kinds of communication skills, like designing of logo, video editing, picture editing, PR managing and photography with ensuring our communication should be effective and to bring about positive social impact on audiences.



2. Performing arts

Volunteers who can support children and youth in the pursuit and attainments of arts, dance, singing, handcrafts etc.

Volunteers with having basic knowledge of primary education from class 1st to 10th, who can help us in teaching and re-creative involvement of teaching methods.


4. Indoors and outdoors sports

Volunteers assisting in easy indoor activities for kids, like chess, ludo, Quiz or inexpensive hand-made games that are easy to play in slum areas to refresh their brain.
