ABOUT US

Anupam Bharat Sangh NGO is a non-profit organization, dedicated to help underprivileged people around our area South west Delhi. We work on providing education, health care facilities, corrective surgeries and helping and distributing the required items. Along with mental and environmental awareness programs. Our volunteers are well disposed and available to provide any services 24/7. We work on overall aspects of society to make one’s life better and healthy.
Vision- So far we have enrolled many underprivileged kids in government school and sponsored their education expenses. Also, we frequently arrange healthcare camps in slum areas for routine check-up of these underprivileged peoples. We try our best to make their necessities fulfilled. During this Covid-19 crisis we had distributed raw food items and other necessary stuffs to the peoples who were in need. Mission- we want to provide education to as many underprivileged kids possible. No kid should deprived of education due to poverty or lack of resources. Women should be financially independent and free from any kind of violence and abuse. Environmental awareness programs to make surrounding better and it’s preservation.

AIM AND OBJECTIVES
1. Our paramount focus is on imparting education to each and every youth and adult.

2. Promote Women empowerment.

3. Create awareness regarding mental health issues.

4. Take necessary and suitable action against any kind of violence occurred either on women, children or old age individuals.
